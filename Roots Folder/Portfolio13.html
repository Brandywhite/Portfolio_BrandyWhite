This page was a mockup of a bulletin board that was supposed to go with the other two zoo posters. We had much more leeway with this as the only criteria was that it had to fit the size of a Billboard. As the San Diego zoo was working on their polar Bear exhibit so I decided to run with that. I had a bit of trouble creating a slogan for my billboard but once I decided on Summer just got cooler, the fun in the sun looking for the billboard was easy to design.
